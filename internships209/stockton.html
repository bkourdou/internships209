Why Stockton Students Have Real Opportunities

Stockton is the seat of San Joaquin County — one of California's most economically diverse regions. With a population of nearly 320,000 and a growing public-sector workforce, Stockton offers meaningful student internships that aren't just resume fillers.

The City of Stockton alone hires interns across departments including IT, Public Works, Economic Development, HR, and Finance. The Port of Stockton — one of California's largest inland ports — offers hands-on logistics and public administration experience. State agencies like Caltrans District 10 and the CA Dept. of Food & Agriculture operate out of Stockton too.

For students interested in healthcare, Sutter Health's Central Valley hospitals offer clinical education internships. And programs like SJC WorkNet and Eckerd Connects provide paid work experience with wraparound support for students facing barriers.
