Modesto's Unique Student Opportunity Landscape

Modesto is the seat of Stanislaus County and one of the most economically productive agricultural regions in the world. Home to E. & J. Gallo Winery — the world's largest family-owned wine company — Modesto offers students rare access to global-scale employers right in their backyard.

The Bay Valley Tech free coding bootcamp and paid tech internship program is unique in the state: students receive 4 months of free technical training followed by 28 weeks of paid work at $25/hr. No other program in the 209 matches this model for people aged 16–30 looking to break into tech.

The City of Modesto offers paid and volunteer internships year-round across all departments. Stanislaus County provides semester-based internships at $16.50–$20.07/hr. The CA Dept. of Food & Agriculture's Modesto field office offers flexible year-round internships open to all college majors.
