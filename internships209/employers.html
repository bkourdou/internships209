Why post on Internships209?

We're different from LinkedIn, Indeed, and other big job boards — and that's the point.

🆓

Always Free

LinkedIn charges $300+/month. Indeed charges per click. We charge nothing. Internships209 is free for every employer — nonprofits, small businesses, school districts, and government agencies.

🎯

The Right Audience

Every visitor is a student or parent in the 209 area, actively looking for internship opportunities. No irrelevant applications from out-of-state or experienced professionals who don't qualify.

✅

Vetted & Trusted

Every listing is reviewed before it goes live. Students know this site only shows real, legitimate opportunities — which means they take applications seriously.

⚡

Live in 24 Hours

Submit the form and your listing is usually live the same day. No waiting on approval queues, no account setup, no contracts to sign.

🌎

Bilingual by Design

The site is built for the 209 community — including Spanish-speaking families. Your listing reaches students and parents who speak both English and Spanish.

📱

Mobile-First

Over 60% of students find internships on their phone. Internships209 is designed mobile-first so your listing looks great on every screen size.

How it works

Three steps. Five minutes.

1

Fill out the listing form

Takes about 5 minutes. Tell us the role, pay, eligibility requirements, deadline, and a link where students can apply. No account needed.

2

We review and publish

We check that the listing is a real, student-appropriate opportunity and publish it — usually within 24 hours. You'll get a confirmation email when it's live.

3

Students apply directly to you

Applicants click "Apply Now" and go straight to your website or email. No middleman, no application portal, no third-party platform fees.
